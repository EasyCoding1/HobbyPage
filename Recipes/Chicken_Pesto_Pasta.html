Chicken Pesto Pasta!

This pesto pasta is a delicious way to get you going.

Learn how to make this dish:

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Chicken
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Parmigianno Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation:

  1. Begin to fry the Chicken
  2. Add Spaghetti
  3. Add everything else
  4. Enjoy!
