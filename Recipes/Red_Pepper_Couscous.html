Red Pepper Couscous!

This Red Pepper Couscous is a delicious and healthy dish that is easy to make.

Learn how to make this dish:

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup of couscous
  • 2 tablespoons of pesto sauce
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Preparation:

  1. 1- Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. 2- Add the diced red bell pepper and saut√© for 5 minutes until it begins to soften.
  3. 3- Add the couscous and stir to coat with the olive oil and red pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. 4- Add the water, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan and bring to a boil.
  5. 5- Lower the heat to a simmer, cover the saucepan, and cook for 10-12 minutes until the water has been absorbed and the couscous is tender.
  6. 6- Remove the saucepan from the heat and fluff the couscous with a fork.
  7. 7- Add the chopped spinach, pesto sauce, and grated Parmesan cheese to the couscous and mix well.
  8. 8- Cover the saucepan and let it sit for 5 minutes to allow the spinach to wilt and the cheese to melt.
  9. 9- Uncover the saucepan and give the couscous a final stir before serving.
  10. 10- Enjoy!
