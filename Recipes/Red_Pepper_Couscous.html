Red Pepper Couscous!

This Red Pepper Couscous is a delicious way to get you going.

Learn how to make this dish:

Ingredients:

  • Red Pepper
  • Couscous
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Parmigianno Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation:

  1. 1. Begin to fry the Pepper
  2. 2. Add Couscous
  3. 3. Add everything else
  4. 4. Enjoy!
